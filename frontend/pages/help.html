Visual Onboarding Guide

Welcome to FlowNest! Master your workspace and grow your focus tree in 9 interactive steps.

Step 1

Create your first task

Navigate to the Tasks tab, type what needs to be done, select priority, and press Enter to save.

Step 2

Add due dates and priorities

Set deadlines and urgency levels (Low/Medium/High) to structure your daily agenda and sync your calendar view.

Step 3

Complete tasks to earn XP

Tick off items on your list to level up your status, gain XP bonuses, and earn virtual currency coins.

Step 4

Grow your Focus Tree

Maintain productivity to watch your seed grow into a stem, branches, leaves, and eventually reach a full flower bloom.

Step 5

Build streaks

Finish tasks daily to increment your consecutive productivity streak and receive premium shop tokens.

Step 6

Configure reminders

Manage your notification settings under settings tab to establish custom email, browser, and push alerts.

Step 7

Track with Analytics

Audit your completion metrics, priority distributions, and productivity stats in the dashboard analytics.

Step 8

Use Stress Games

Take mindful breaks with short, calming games like deep breathing or color tapping directly inside your panel.

Step 9

Use AI Assistant

Consult the Gemini AI helper for schedule recommendations, code planning, or sub-task breakdowns.

Frequently Asked Questions

To create your first task, navigate to the Tasks tab in the sidebar. Click the input field at the top, enter a title, choose a priority level (High, Medium, Low), add optional tags, set a due date, and hit Enter or click the plus button. Your task will appear instantly in the pending list.

The Calendar View (accessible via Calendar in the sidebar or view toggles) automatically pulls all tasks that have a dueDate configured. You can inspect tasks directly on their respective calendar blocks, making it easy to see upcoming deadlines at a glance.

Yes, absolutely. Hover over any task in your task list to expose control buttons. Click the edit icon to change the task's title, priority, tags, or due date. To delete a task, click the trash icon. Completed tasks can also be deleted directly from the history panel.

The Focus Tree (visible on the **Dashboard**) is a visual representation of your daily productivity. Completing tasks in your backlog fills your growth progress bar. As your completion rate rises, the tree grows through six visual stages: Seed, Stem, Small Branches, Tree Structure, Leaves, and full Flower Bloom.

When you achieve 100% task completion, your Focus Tree reaches full bloom. This rewards you with +100 Coins, +25 XP, and increments your **Daily Streak** by 1. A success banner will display at the bottom of the card to celebrate your momentum.

The tree progress is derived from the ratio of completed-to-pending tasks. If you add new tasks to your queue, the percentage of completed tasks will drop, causing the tree to change back to an earlier stage. Complete these new tasks to regrow your tree to full bloom!

XP (Experience Points) are earned by completing tasks (High priority grants more XP than Low priority) and achieving a full Focus Tree bloom. Once your XP bar hits 100 points, you automatically level up and unlock a new title badge displayed on your sidebar user card.

Daily Streaks represent your consistency. You maintain and increment your streak by completing at least one task or achieving a Focus Tree bloom every calendar day. If a full day passes without any task completion activity, your streak resets back to 0.

Navigate to the Settings page. Under the **Notifications** card, you will find toggles for In-App / Browser Reminders, Email Notifications, and Push Notifications. Enabling these switches immediately syncs your preferences with the database.

First, verify that **Email Notifications** is toggled ON in your Settings tab. Second, make sure your task has a valid `dueDate` configured. Reminders are dispatched strictly at offsets: 3 days, 2 days, 1 day, 3 hours, and 1 hour before due dates. Check your spam folder if configuration is correct.

Yes. You can toggle off any specific reminder channel (Email, Browser, or Push) on the Settings page. This will immediately stop reminders on those channels while preserving your user session and tasks.

The Analytics page tracks your total tasks completed, pending backlog counts, productivity distribution by priority level (High/Medium/Low), task category tagging statistics, and completion rate percentages over time.

The AI Assistant (powered by Google Gemini) can analyze your schedule and suggest task prioritizations. You can chat with it to break down large projects, schedule tasks automatically, and structure your daily workflow in real-time.

Profile details are managed on the Settings page under the **Profile Details** card. You can modify your display name or email address and click "Save Changes" to commit the updates directly to the database.
